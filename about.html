About Us

Learn more about Friendly Petz and our passion for pets

Our Story

Friendly Petz was founded with a simple mission: to provide the best quality pet products and supplies to pet owners in Durbanville and surrounding areas. We understand that pets are family members, and they deserve the best care possible.

Since our establishment, we have been dedicated to offering a wide range of pet products, from premium food to toys, accessories, and everything in between. Our team consists of passionate pet lovers who are always ready to help you find the perfect products for your furry, feathered, or finned friends.
